Instructions

Bugs will appear on the left side of the playing screen in random locations, and make their way to the right side of the playing screen. Each time a bug is squashed, your score will increase.

If a bug reaches the right side of the playing screen without getting squashed, you will lose. There are three different types of bugs; harder bugs are worth more points but more difficult to swat. The game will become harder the longer you play.
